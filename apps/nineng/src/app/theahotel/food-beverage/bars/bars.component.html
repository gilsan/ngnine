Dining

Discover a variety of exquisite dining experiences andexceptional service at Park Hyatt Seoul’s restaurants and bars.Using only the freshest, highest-quality ingredients, the chefsat Park Hyatt Seoul create savory, palate-pleasing cuisine withflair and creativity. Located on the 2nd floor, our main restaurant, Cornerstone,offers authentic Italian dishes, a vast array of premiumbeverages and a convivial and relaxing atmosphere. Offeringpanoramic views of Seoul, The Lounge, on the 24th floor, servesmodern Korean cuisine specially created for the enjoyment ofKoreans and foreigners alike, as well as a wide selection ofbeverages including traditional Korean teas and cocktails. TheTimber House, on the lower level floor, is a vinyl music bar,presenting Japanese cuisine and premium beverages.
