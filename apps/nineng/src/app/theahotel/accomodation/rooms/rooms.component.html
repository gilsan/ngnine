Presidential Suite

Step into the embodiment of luxury, amid captivating views of Gangnam. On the 22nd floor of Park Hyatt Seoul, discover an expansive 160-square meter suite combining traditional Korean aesthetics and contemporary inspiration. Enjoy sound sleep in a bedroom with a king bed and recliner, a spacious bathroom evoking spa luxury, and a generously-appointed living room adorned with traditional Korean patterns and furniture, complemented by a kitchen, dressing room, minibar and guest bathroom. This is a premium suite. See World of Hyatt program terms for upgrade eligibility.
